EXAMPLE: WHAT THE CODE DOES
================================================================================

Input Question:
  "What are the symptoms of diabetes?"

Process:
  1. Tokenize: [What, are, the, symptoms, of, diabetes]
  2. Embed: Convert to 64-dimensional vectors
  3. Compute attention: Which tokens relate to which?
  4. Analyze: Generate scores and interpretations

Output (Attention Matrix):
        What   are    the   symptoms    of   diabetes
What   0.16   0.08   0.22    0.19     0.20   0.15
are    0.18   0.26   0.13    0.14     0.12   0.18
the    0.12   0.13   0.20    0.22     0.14   0.18
symptoms 0.09  0.09   0.14    0.32     0.26   0.11
of     0.19   0.18   0.16    0.20     0.14   0.14
diabetes 0.16  0.19   0.21    0.11     0.13   0.21

Analysis:
  - "symptoms" has high attention to itself (0.32) - key entity
  - "symptoms" attends to "of" (0.26) - grammatical relation
  - "diabetes" has high self-attention (0.21) - important entity
  - Decoder uses this to match question to passage about diabetes symptoms

Answer Generated:
  "Increased thirst, urination, fatigue"
